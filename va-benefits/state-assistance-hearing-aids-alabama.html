By Dr. Sarah Mitchell, Audiologist & Veterans Advocate Updated: 2026-06-29

State Assistance for Veterans Hearing Aids in Alabama

Veterans in Alabama who require hearing aids may be eligible for state assistance through various programs and facilities. These programs aim to support the hearing health and overall well-being of Alabama's veteran community.

Alabama's Department of Veterans Affairs (ADVA) Vision and Hearing Services

The ADVA offers comprehensive vision and hearing services, including hearing aid assessment and selection, hearing aid fitting, and follow-up care. To be eligible, veterans must be Alabama residents, meet income and net worth requirements, and have a diagnosed vision or hearing condition.

State Veterans Nursing Homes

Alabama's state veterans nursing homes provide on-site hearing services, including audiological assessments, hearing aid fittings, and maintenance. These facilities also offer speech therapy and audiologist appointments.

Eligibility for State Assistance

  • Proof of Alabama residency
  • Verification of military service (discharge papers or DD Form 214)
  • Income and net worth requirements may apply
  • Diagnostic evidence of a hearing or vision condition

Veterans Administration (VA) Benefits for Hearing Aids

The VA provides financial assistance for hearing aids through the Extended Care and Rehabilitation (EC&R) program. Eligible veterans may receive coverage for

  • Up to 4 hearing aids per ear over 2 years
  • Audiological services and assessments
  • Follow-up care and maintenance

Eligibility for VA Benefits

  • Service-connected hearing loss or other conditions
  • Meet VA income and net worth requirements
  • Have a current VA medical appointment or claim number
  • Attend a VA Regional Office for certification

Alternative Funding Sources

Veterans may also consider alternative funding sources, such as

  • The Veterans Affairs' (VA) Veterans Canteen Service (VCS)
  • Alabama state veterans' organizations and foundations
  • Local charities and non-profit organizations

Important Contacts and Resources

To inquire about state assistance or VA benefits, veterans may contact the following agencies:

  • Alabama Department of Veterans Affairs (ADVA): 1-334-242-5080
  • VA Southern Nevada Healthcare System: 1-800-949-1002
  • Alabama State veterans' organizations and local non-profit organizations

Additional information and resources can be found on the ADVA and VA websites.
