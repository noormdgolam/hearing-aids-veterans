Resources for Veteran-Friendly Audiologists in Hawaii

Veterans who are experiencing hearing loss or other auditory issues in Hawaii may be eligible for free or low-cost hearing aids through the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs (VA).

The VA offers a range of services and benefits to assist veterans with hearing impairments, including hearing aids, audiological assessments, and cochlear implants. However, navigating the process and finding the right audiologist and facilities can be daunting.

This resource guide provides information on where to find veteran-friendly audiologists and facilities in Hawaii that offer access to VA benefits and resources.

Veteran Eligibility and VA Benefits

Eligibility for VA benefits and services, including hearing aids, is typically tied to service-connected disabilities. Veterans who served during wartime periods and have a service-connected disability rating of 10% or higher may be eligible for hearing aids and other audiological services.

To qualify, veterans must submit an application to the VA, including documentation of their military service and a claim for compensation for hearing loss or impairment.
