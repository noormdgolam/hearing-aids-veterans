Finding Veteran-Friendly Audiologists in New Hampshire

As a veteran, accessing quality healthcare services, including audiology services, is crucial for maintaining overall well-being. In New Hampshire, veterans can take advantage of various resources and benefits to seek professional help for their hearing concerns.

VA Benefits and Hearing Loss

  • The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) offers compensation benefits to veterans with service-connected hearing loss or tinnitus.
  • To be eligible, veterans must have a disability rating for hearing loss or tinnitus and provide supporting medical documentation.

Locating Veteran-Friendly Audiologists in New Hampshire

Audiologists certified by the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association (ASHA) can provide excellent care for veterans. To find a veteran-friendly audiologist in New Hampshire, consider the following steps:

  • Check the VA Website: Visit the VA Health Care website (www.va.gov/health-care/) and use their Provider Finder tool to locate audiologists who accept VA insurance.
  • Contact Local VA Offices: Reach out to your local VA office to ask for recommendations on audiologists in your area.
  • Get Referrals from Veterans Organizations: Organizations like the Veterans of Foreign Wars (VFW) and Disabled American Veterans (DAV) often have a list of recommended audiologists for veterans.
  • Verify Certification and Qualifications: Ensure the audiologist is certified by ASHA and has experience working with veterans with hearing loss or tinnitus.

VA Benefits and Payment Options

As a veteran with hearing loss, you may be eligible for various benefits, including:

  • Compensation and Pension: VA compensation and pension benefits may help cover the cost of audiology services, including hearing aids.
  • VA Insurance: Veterans can use their VA insurance to pay for audiology services at participating providers.
  • State-specific Assistance: Some states, like New Hampshire, offer additional assistance programs for veterans, including hearing aid funding.

Next Steps

Once you've found a veteran-friendly audiologist, schedule an appointment to discuss your hearing concerns and explore available benefits. Don't hesitate to ask questions about:

  • Diagnostic Services: What tests will be performed to diagnose the extent of hearing loss?
  • Treatment Options: What types of hearing aids or other treatment options are available?
  • VA Benefits and Payment Options: How can I access and use my VA benefits to cover audiology services?

By following these steps and seeking out veteran-friendly audiologists in New Hampshire, veterans can access quality audiology services and make informed decisions about their hearing health.
