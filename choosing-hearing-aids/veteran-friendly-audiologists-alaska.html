Accessing Hearing Care Services for Veterans in Alaska

As a veteran in Alaska, access to high-quality hearing care services is crucial for maintaining overall health and well-being.

The U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) offers various benefits, including hearing aids and audiological services, to eligible veterans.

This informative section will guide you through the process of finding veteran-friendly audiologists in Alaska and navigating VA benefits for hearing aids and audiological services.

Eligibility for VA Hearing Benefits

The VA provides various hearing benefits, including:

  • Hearing aid benefits through the VA Prosthetic and Sensory Aids Service
  • Audiological services, including diagnostic testing and treatment
  • Communication devices and other assistive listening devices

To be eligible for VA hearing benefits, veterans must meet specific requirements:

  • Have military service-connected hearing loss or tinnitus
  • Have non-military service-related hearing loss or tinnitus, with a 10% or higher VA rating
  • Have a service-related disability rating of 10% or higher

Finding Veteran-Friendly Audiologists in Alaska

The VA recommends the following options to find a veteran-friendly audiologist in Alaska:

  1. Visit VHA Audiologic and Speech Pathology Services by State to find a local VA audiologic clinic. You can visit a VA clinic for an initial evaluation and to discuss your hearing care options.
  2. Search the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association's Find a Professional directory for audiologists in your area.
  3. Contact the Alaska VA Regional Office for recommendations on hearing care providers in the state.

Additional Resources

For more information on VA hearing benefits and services, visit:

  • VA Hearing Loss and Tinnitus website
  • Alaska VA Regional Office: (800) 827-1000 or akrovet@va.gov

It's essential to work closely with your audiologist and the VA to ensure you receive the care and services you need to address your hearing loss and maintain your quality of life.
